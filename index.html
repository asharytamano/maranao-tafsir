Welcome

This is the official online edition of the Maranao Tafsir of the Holy Qur’an, as translated and interpreted by Ustadh Abdulbasit Tamano.

Our goal is to preserve, present, and propagate the meaning of the Qur’an in the beautiful Maranao language, making it accessible to scholars, youth, and the wider Ummah.

"A language of the heart, a light for the soul."
